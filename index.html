Background


Working at an architectural firm, I was lucky enough to be part of standard development team and to be able to get opportunity to develop scripts to automate design engineering tasks. That changed my whole perspective of how I can speed up the design process by automating tasks like exports, generating pdfs, real time data connection, etc. This sparked a curiosity to learn something new and take the encouragement further into tech industry, which I have always been fond of. Data Science, Machine Learning and AI for me is an amalgamation of many skills like engineering, statistics, computer science and math. The mere idea to tell a meaningful story and change people's lives using data has always been inspiring to me. A good example is Google Maps.
